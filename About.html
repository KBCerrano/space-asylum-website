About
K.B Cerrano

K.B Cerrano is canadian born comic book artist of science fiction and fantasy all with a touch of comedy. Author of his debut science fiction space opera comic book Space Asylum.

Cerrano graduated with an Honours Bsc in Statistics and minor in philosophy from the University of Ottawa, Canada in 2025. His work is inspired by a love of philosophy and science set in fantasy worlds created for all audiences with intellectual comedies- powered by a love of coffee.

The Vision

Space Asylum is my passionate debut project - a comic book series I originally started during the 2019 pandemic (while also studying the perilously annoying proofs of limit theorems).

It’s a science-fiction series that blends action, humor, and philosophical ideas into a loveable story that continues to grow. Join Captain Tom as he travels across universe. From desert planets and crowded neon cities to the orderly bases of the Universal Government and the lawless regions of the outer rim. Meet loveable bantering friends, mysterious enemies with hidden motives, and everything in between.

This is just the beginning!
