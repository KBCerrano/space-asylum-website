Welcome to the Year 2220.

Captain Tom was once the captain of the Sutallite, a renowned space crew, until a mysterious enemy in a golden spacesuit destroyed his mothership. Months later, haunted by the past, Tom embarks on a perilous trek through the cosmos.

His mission is simple yet daunting: re-unite his now separated friends and reforge the crew they once shared. But with deadly foes stalking his every move, Tom will learn that nostalgia can come at a heavy price.
